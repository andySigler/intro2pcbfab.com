Steps to Making a Printed Circuit Board

  • [design]

      • 1: Prototype (the interaction)

      2: Bill of Materials (the parts)

      3: Schematic (the connections)

      4: Layout & Dimension (the shape)

      5: Design Copper (the traces)

  • [production]

      • 1: Cutout & Holes (with micro-mills)

      2: Remove Copper (with micro-mills or acid etching)

      3: Masks, Labels, & Stencils (with UV exposure, toner transfer, & laser cutter)

      4: Populate & Reflow (with solder paste & SMD vacuum pens)

      5: Program (with Arduino & programming jigs)

    • a video of it working
    • a parts list
      • not like individual components, but like "Adafruit ADXL355 Accelerometer" works
    • a hand/illustrator drawing of the connections
