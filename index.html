For next class:

  1. Download Eagle, and I recommend start looking at tutorials online
    • Sparkfun tutorial, ignore "3. How to streamline your EAGLE configuration"
    • Try other tutorials and post good ones on your blog (there's so many)
  2. Create a class category on your blog, and send me the link.
  3. Make a prototype of your project for this class, and make a short blog post with the following:
    • a 1-5 sentance description of the final device
    • a video of it working
    • a parts list
      • not like individual components, but like "Adafruit ADXL355 Accelerometer" works
    • a hand/illustrator drawing of the connections
    • the code it uses (if any)
